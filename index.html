Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common consultancy and pathway inquiries.

What is the typical processing time?

Processing times vary by destination and pathway. Generally, student visas take 3-5 months, while professional pathways can take 6-12 months depending on document verification and employer processes.

What are the basic requirements?

For skilled workers, you generally need a valid passport, proof of relevant experience, and sometimes educational certifications. For students, a high school or university diploma and proof of funds are required.

What are the cost expectations?

Costs include consultancy fees, visa fees, document translation/attainment, and initial settlement funds. We provide a transparent breakdown during your initial pathway assessment.

What are my visa success chances?

While no one can guarantee a visa, our high success rate comes from thorough eligibility screening. We only proceed with applications where we see a strong probability of success.
